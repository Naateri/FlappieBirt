Contacting us!

Have some cool and swaggy ideas for the game? Would you like your idea to be featured somewhere? An easter egg maybe? We appreciate all your feedback and ideas, so if you want to communicate with us or ask us about anything (literally anything, not only regarding the app; maybe you want some new friends, we can help ;) ), don't be shy about contacting us!!

You can contact us via:

  • Send us an email at SOON with the subjects:
    • "Ideas", if you want to submit an idea to the Flappie Birt Team about the game
    • "Feedback", if you want to give constructive criticism about a certain part of the project.
    • "Random", if you want to send anything else (literally anything).
  • Contact Alejandro Risco personally by:
    • Sending him an email at alejandro.risco@ucsp.edu.pe
    • Take a look at his github.
  • Contact Renato Postigo personally by:
    • Sending him an email at renato.postigo@ucsp.edu.pe
    • Take a look at his github.

Important:

If you are gonna send an email to any of the devs, make sure you specify that the email is regarding this project (or that you found the email because of this webpage). Thank you.
