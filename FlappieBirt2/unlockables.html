Unlockables

What could you possibly unlock??

There are lots of things you can unlock in this world of flappies. For example:

  • Different Backgrounds: we assume you're quite bored of the Mario-looking background that's been there forever.
  • Different characters: we also assume you are bored of the same bird (birt) all the time. (Yes, we know the game is FlappieBIRT, but there's a Doge jumping around some buildings so that makes this game even cooler).
  • Different pipes: in the image at the main page, there's a building instead of the usual pipe. As you can see, our point is to make the game as different and unique as posible compared to the original Flappy Bird.
  • A public leadearboard with the best scores around the world, where you're gonna be able to contribute everytime you die!

You may be wondering "How?" Well it's pretty easy!

In-game currency SHALL be available at a certain point of future development, with which you should be able to buy the different things mentioned above.

For now, you'll be able to try the couple of different skins that have been designed so far (missing a design team *sadface*).
